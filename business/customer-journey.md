# Customer Journey Map for Cost-Optimizer

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: B2C SaaS companies notice rising variable costs in their monthly expenses due to AI API usage.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about cost optimization solutions; difficulty in identifying the exact cost drivers.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration and confusion about managing costs; anxiety over profitability.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide informative content (blogs, webinars) on cost management; offer free cost assessment tools to highlight potential savings.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and content downloads related to cost optimization.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Companies actively search for solutions to reduce costs and improve profitability.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by the number of available solutions; skepticism about the effectiveness of cost optimization tools.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful but cautious; desire for clarity and assurance.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer case studies and testimonials showcasing successful cost reductions; provide a comparison tool to evaluate different solutions.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and engagement with case studies.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Companies sign up for a free trial or demo of the cost-optimizer platform.
- **Friction Points**: Complexity in initial setup; potential technical issues during integration with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with apprehension; eagerness to see results but worried about implementation challenges.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide personalized onboarding support; create easy-to-follow setup guides and video tutorials.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user engagement during the trial period.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Companies decide to purchase the cost-optimizer platform after trial.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about ongoing costs vs. savings; uncertainty about long-term ROI.
- **User Emotions**: Relief and satisfaction from making a decision; cautious optimism about future savings.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer a clear ROI calculator; provide a dedicated account manager for ongoing support and strategy.
- **Metric**: Customer retention rate and time to first significant savings realization.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Companies experience initial success and consider additional features or services.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about which additional features will provide the most value; potential budget constraints.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence in the solution but cautious about further investment; excitement about potential growth.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide tailored recommendations for feature expansions based on usage data; offer loyalty discounts for long-term customers.
- **Metric**: Average revenue per user (ARPU) and number of upsell opportunities converted.
