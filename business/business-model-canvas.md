# Business Model Canvas for Cost Optimizer

## Customer Segments
- B2C SaaS companies seeking to reduce variable costs associated with AI API usage.
- Startups and small businesses with limited budgets for AI services.
- Enterprises looking for scalable solutions to optimize operational costs.

## Value Propositions
- Reduce variable costs per user by optimizing AI API usage.
- Provide alternative solutions to expensive AI services, potentially saving up to 30% on costs.
- User-friendly dashboard for real-time cost tracking and optimization recommendations.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the company website and targeted online marketing campaigns.
- Partnerships with SaaS incubators and accelerators to reach startups.
- Online webinars and workshops to demonstrate the platform's capabilities.

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated customer support for onboarding and ongoing assistance.
- Community forums for users to share best practices and optimization tips.
- Regular updates and newsletters to keep customers informed about new features and cost-saving strategies.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: Monthly fee of $99 (approx. 3,500 THB) for basic access; $299 (approx. 10,500 THB) for premium features.
- Performance-based pricing: Additional fees based on the percentage of cost savings achieved.
- Consulting services for tailored optimization strategies, priced at $150/hour (approx. 5,300 THB).

## Key Resources
- Proprietary algorithms for AI API usage optimization.
- Development team skilled in SaaS and cost optimization technologies.
- Marketing and sales team to drive customer acquisition and retention.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and improvement of the cost optimization algorithms.
- Customer acquisition through targeted marketing and partnerships.
- Data analysis to refine optimization strategies and enhance user experience.

## Key Partners
- AI API providers for integration and collaboration on cost-saving solutions.
- SaaS industry associations for networking and market insights.
- Financial consultants to validate cost-saving claims and enhance credibility.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: Salaries for engineers and data scientists (~$200,000/year or ~7,200,000 THB).
- Marketing expenses: Budget of $50,000/year (approx. 1,800,000 THB) for online campaigns and partnerships.
- Customer support costs: Salaries and tools for support staff (~$80,000/year or ~2,880,000 THB).
