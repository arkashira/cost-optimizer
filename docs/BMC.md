# Business Model Canvas (BMC)
=====================================

## Value Proposition
-------------------

Our cost optimization platform helps B2C SaaS companies reduce their variable costs per user by:

* Optimizing AI API usage through machine learning-based analysis and prediction
* Providing alternative solutions to reduce costs, such as:
	+ AI API usage reduction through optimized model selection and configuration
	+ Alternative AI API providers with competitive pricing and quality
	+ Customized cost-saving strategies based on individual company needs

By leveraging our platform, B2C SaaS companies can:

* Reduce costs associated with AI API usage
* Improve profitability and competitiveness
* Enhance customer satisfaction through better resource allocation

## Customer Segments
-------------------

Our primary customer segments are:

* B2C SaaS companies with high variable costs associated with AI API usage
* Companies with large user bases and high AI API usage volumes
* Companies seeking to optimize their AI API usage and reduce costs

## Channels
------------

We will reach our customers through:

* Online marketing and advertising (e.g. Google Ads, social media)
* Content marketing (e.g. blog posts, whitepapers, webinars)
* Partnerships with industry associations and organizations
* Referrals and word-of-mouth

## Revenue Streams
------------------

Our primary revenue streams are:

* Subscription-based model: customers pay a monthly or annual fee for access to our platform
* Transactional fees: customers pay a fee for each AI API usage optimization or alternative solution implemented through our platform
* Premium services: customers pay for additional features, support, or customization
